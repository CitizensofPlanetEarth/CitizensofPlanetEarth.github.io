Mission Statement



The mission of “The Citizens of the Planet Earth” is to bring about
peace and cooperation among all people inhabiting ‘The Planet Earth.’


Our goal is to bring about a mutual respect for all human beings on the “Planet Earth” by having each induvial person agree to and become knowledgeable of the United Nations “Universal Declaration of Inalienable Human Rights.”
The individual person would then contractually sign and agree to all or a select few of the U.N. listed Inalienable Human Rights, that they are willing to uphold in kind to their fellow “Citizens of the Planet Earth” and thus becomes a “Citizen of the Planet Earth”.
To further our commitment of respect for one another’s inalienable rights “The Citizens of the Planet Earth” shall institute and maintain a secure planet earth
Electronic opinion polls to record their opinion on local, state, national and international.
Hopefully, our world leaders will take notice of the "Will of the People" and learn to use peaceful means to resolve conflicts without armed conflict. Through this worldwide opinion poll, humanity will be able to make known the true "Will of The Citizens of Planet Earth.”

Goals of the "Citizens of the Planet" Earth are:


1.Universal Recognition of Inalienable Human Rights of Every Individual: Person On The Planet Earth; Thus empowering humanity to universaly respect all race,colors,creeds,nationalities, any other human distinction and promote that we are all equal and responsible to eachother, in "Inalienable Human Rights”* that they are willing to extend and uphold to each and every individual person on “The Planet Earth”.

2. Individual Opinion Poll Recording: To institute and maintain a secure electronic opinion poll which has recording capability for all “The Citizens of Planet Earth” to record their opinion on local, state, national and international legislation and concerns.
This personal individual opinion poll record can be changed in the future if additional facts and information necessitate a change of their recorded opinion on a specific concern.

3.Individual Responsibility: To bring about an awareness to each and every individual person inhabiting "the planet earth" that they have a responsibility to their creator, themselves, their children and their fellow “Citizens of the Planet Earth” to uphold “Universal Inalienable Human Rights”* for humanity to register their opinion daily on local, state, national, and international concerns.
This recording of each individual citizen’s opinion shall by majority opinion, direct and guide our leaders and officials in THE TRUE WILL OF THE PEOPLE.

Plan of action for World Peace by “Citizens of the Planet Earth”


“The Citizens of the Planet Earth” plan for the world peace is to provide a secure Planet Earth communication forum for all humanity:

1. Every human being anywhere on the ‘Planet Earth’ can identify and uphold "The UN "Universal Declataion of Inalienable Human Rights" universally acceptable to each and every induvial person inhabiting the planet earth.

2. The individual personal records of what inalienable human rights they will uphold, refuse to uphold or hold in abeyance to their fellow human beings inhabiting “The Planet Earth” are their personal secure website station.

3. The individual person becomes a “Citizen of the Planet Earth” by pledging to uphold the “Inalienable Human Rights” they accepted to each and every “Citizen of The Planet Earth” and likewise expect their fellow citizens to uphold in return to them.

4. The “Citizen of the Planet Earth” pledges their reciprocal consent to respect the Alienable human rights* of a majority of the citizens in any self governing society.

5. The “New Citizen of the Planet Earth” pledges to record their opinion weekly on local,state, national and worldwide concerns to direct our elected leaders with their international recorded opinion poll expressing the “true will of the people” they serve.

*Alienable Rights: local laws and customs the majority of citizens agree to uphold to eachother for peace, harmony and the mutual respect of the local inhabitants for sharing commerce and propertry.
